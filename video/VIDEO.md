# 🎬 Sentinel Protocol — Video Pitch (8-Slide)

**Target:** ~3–4 minutes
**Style:** Calm, confident, slightly slower than your instinct

---

## Slide 1 — The Problem

**Intro:**
"Let’s start with the problem."

**Description:**
Open with urgency.

"AI agents today operate with unlimited power and zero accountability."

Then walk through the stats:

"Two days ago, Drift Protocol lost $280 million — not because of a bug, but because of a governance failure."

"Meanwhile, Solana has already processed over 15 million transactions from AI agents."

"And yet — there are zero standardized protocols to control what these agents can actually do."

Pause briefly.

"That’s the gap we’re solving."

⏱️ ~25–30s

---

## Slide 2 — The Solution

**Intro:**
"So we built Sentinel Protocol."

**Description:**
Deliver your one-liner clearly:

"Sentinel is the open-source governance standard for AI agents on Solana."

Then explain simply:

"It defines how agents are controlled, limited, and audited on-chain."

Walk through features conversationally:

- "Permissions — what an agent is allowed to access"
- "Circuit breakers — automatic kill-switches when something goes wrong"
- "Audit trail — every action logged on-chain"
- "Spending policies — programmable limits and controls"

Then anchor with code:

"All of this is implemented in just a few lines of code."

⏱️ ~25–30s

---

## Slide 3 — Why Solana

**Intro:**
"This only works because of Solana."

**Description:**

"Solana gives us sub-400 millisecond finality — meaning governance decisions happen in real time."

"Fees are less than a fraction of a cent — so it's economically viable to govern thousands of agents."

"With Anchor and PDAs, we can build composable, secure governance primitives."

"And most importantly — this is where AI agents already live."

"Over 15 million agent transactions have already happened here."

Optional emphasis:

"We’re not betting on the future — we’re building where the present already exists."

⏱️ ~25s

---

## Slide 4 — Market

**Intro:**
"Let’s look at the opportunity."

**Description:**

"The global agentic economy is projected at $47 billion by 2028."

"On Solana alone, that’s an $8.2 billion segment."

Then show your thinking:

"If just 5% of agents adopt governance — that’s 25,000 paying agents."

"At an average of $1,370 per month, that’s a $410 million annual market."

Then anchor:

"And our users are clear — agent developers, DAOs, and DeFi protocols already interacting with these systems."

⏱️ ~25–30s

---

## Slide 5 — Business Model

**Intro:**
"Here’s how we capture value."

**Description:**

"40% comes from protocol fees — a tiny fee per governed transaction."

"35% from premium features — advanced policies, analytics, and insurance."

"25% from validator fees — third parties auditing agent behavior."

Then simplify:

"As adoption grows, revenue scales naturally with the number of governed agents."

⏱️ ~20–25s

---

## Slide 6 — Differentiator

**Intro:**
"We’re not the first to see this problem — but no one has solved it end-to-end."

**Description:**

Move quickly:

- "Agent Registry gives identity — but no control"
- "SAID handles discovery — but no governance"
- "ElizaOS and GOAT build agents — but no guardrails"

Then contrast:

"Sentinel combines everything — permissions, circuit breakers, audit trails, and governance — into one standard."

Then deliver the strategic insight:

"Metaplex didn’t win by building a product — it won by becoming the standard."

"That’s exactly what we’re doing for agent governance."

⏱️ ~25–30s

---

## Slide 7 — Roadmap

**Intro:**
"Here’s how we execute."

**Description:**

Walk through phases quickly:

"We start with a working MVP on devnet — already live."

"Then SDK and demo — enabling developers to integrate in just a few lines."

"Next, integrations — ElizaOS, GOAT, and real agents using Sentinel."

"And finally — mainnet, audit, and scaling to 100+ governed agents."

Then reinforce validation:

"Our goal is simple: prove real usage, not just build technology."

⏱️ ~20–25s

---

## Slide 8 — Team & Closing

**Intro:**
"And we’re the team to build this."

**Description:**

"Deep experience in Solana, Anchor, and smart contract security."

"We understand both the technical layer — and the governance gap."

Then close strong:

"If AI agents will control billions in on-chain assets…"

Pause.

"Someone needs to define the rules."

Short pause.

"That’s Sentinel Protocol."

Then:

"Thank you."

⏱️ ~20–25s
